Founding and Early Ambitions

Po Kiara Mas was founded in 2024, with the ambition to provide safe, comfortable, affordable and environmentally friendly transportation services. Starting with a fleet of five buses, the company is run with a passion to make a real difference in the land transportation industry.

Expansion and Diversification of Services

Following this success, we expanded our route network and began offering private charter services as well as partnerships with travel agents for integrated holiday packages. With increasingly diversified services, PO Kiara Mas grew exponentially, doubling the number of fleets and routes within two years.

Achievement and Recognition

The highlight of our journey comes in 2028, when PO Kiara Mas is awarded the “Most Innovative Transportation Company” award for our efforts in environmentally friendly technology and outstanding customer service. This recognition strengthens our reputation and encourages more passengers to choose PO Kiara Mas as their travel partner.
